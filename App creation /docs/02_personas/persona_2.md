## Persona 2: Théo

**Name:** Théo

**Age:** 22 years old

**Situation:** Computer Science student, lives with his parents.

**Biography:** Théo is a competitive player who loves to "tryhard" in League of Legends and Valorant. He also enjoys sharing strategies and tips he discovers with his community.

**Goals:**

- To join a small team to practice (scrim) in Valorant.

**Pain points:**
