## Persona 4: Clément

**Name:** Clément

**Age:** 53 years old

**Situation:** IT engineer, divorced.

**Biography:** Clément plays video games often. He has been passionate about them since he was young, especially single-player games.

**Goals:**
- To find the single-player game of his dreams.

**Pain points:**
- Young, toxic players.
- The high price of games.
